Confidentiality

Confidentiality between lawyers and clients is a fundamental and crucial aspect of the attorney-client relationship. This confidentiality, often referred to as attorney-client privilege, is designed to protect the open and honest communication between an attorney and their client, allowing clients to share sensitive information with their attorneys without fear of it being disclosed to others.

Availability

Availability between lawyers and clients is a critical aspect of the attorney-client relationship. Effective communication and accessibility are essential for maintaining trust, transparency, and the successful pursuit of legal objectives.

Intergrity

Integrity between lawyers and clients is a fundamental aspect of the attorney-client relationship. It involves ethical and moral principles that guide the behavior of both lawyers and clients to maintain trust and transparency throughout the legal process.
